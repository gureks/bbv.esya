“INDIA lives in its villages”
-Mahatma Gandhi
68.9% of our population lives in rural areas (Census 2011) and the growth rate has sharply declined. Villages in India which are in dire need of attention to enhance their living standards. People struggle just to have 1 meal per day let alone live a comfortable life. As the future of a fast developing nation whose 21.9% population still lives below poverty line, it is our duty and also a necessity to work towards the development of a better India.



EVENT DESCRIPTION

This event aims to bring together students from across the nation to study, analyse and brainstorm about the various problems faced in villages and come up with an implementable solution. Instead of working with NGOs, this event encourages students to directly visit a village and interact with people and get a firsthand experience and awareness about the problems faced by rural India. Participants are expected to go through the various policies by the Govt of India which are rolled out for the development of rural areas. With their own understanding of the problem along with the currently present solutions/policies, participants should work on an optimal solution.
